HOW I CAN HELP



Restring


Having the right string and tension can either make or break your game. At Ascend, we specialize in precision stringing that maximizes on power, control, feel, and tension maintenance. Experience peak performance on the court with our state of the art stringing machine and tour level experience. We offer restrings on tennis, squash, and badminton racquets.



Weight & Balance


Think all racquets are made equal? Not quite. When you buy two or more of the same racquet, there are manufacturing variances, meaning your static weight, swingweight, and balance can be different than the specifications written on the frame. At Ascend, we match your racquets so all your frames are the same specs.



Coffee


Would you like to make the most of your time while your racquet is being restrung? Indulge in the pleasure of a complimentary cup of coffee on us. Our crafted beverages start with the finest freshly roasted and ground beans, ensuring a delightful experience in every sip. Our specialty lies in meticulously prepared espresso-based coffees, inspired by the rich coffee culture of Melbourne.
