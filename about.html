Hi, I'm Calvin

I string racquets and I like to make latte art!

My Story

Based in Markham, ON, I've been stringing professionally for over 5 years and have worked with players of all levels, from beginners to professionals. Some notable players I have strung for are:

  • Matteo Berrettini (ATP highest ranked #6 in January 2022)
  • Jason Kubler (Australian Open Doubles champion 2023)
  • Yoshihito Nishioka (ATP highest ranked #24 in June 2023)
  • Coco Vandeweghe (WTA highest ranked #9 in January 2018)
  • Jaimee Fourlis (Australian Open mixed finalist 2022)
Whether you are a novice player trying to stay active or a professional needing perfect tension/customization, I will give you my best commitment to quality and service. During my spare time, I like to work on my barista skills and practice my latte art. Having living in Melbourne, Australia for almost 4 years, I have grown to love espresso based coffees. If you're not in a hurry, enjoy a cup of coffee from me while I string up your racquet.
